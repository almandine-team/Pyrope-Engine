These are the naming convections for people who makes games on here, or as a reference for anybody who edits the source code:

- Non-constant variables: camelCase
- Constants: UPPERCASE
- Classes and Types: PascalCase
- Functions with action: camelCase
- Functions with return values: lower_case
- Variables of custom types: camelCase
- Defined values: UPPERCASE
- Enums: CamelCase
